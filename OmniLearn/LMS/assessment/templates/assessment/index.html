Check your Stress & overall Well-being Score!

We invite you to take this self-assessment to help gauge your overall level of Stress, Emotional & Mental Wellbeing.

Stress may be manifesting in various ways in different aspects of your life, sometimes consciously, sometimes unconsciously. It could be in the way you hold your body, in the way you react to people, in your emotions and in other less visible ways.

This assessment would allow you to assess your current areas of concern, if any, and establish where you can start your learning journey from.

The Assessment will take about 10-15 minutes to complete. It's an optional but a highly recommended 1st step to start your journey.

The results of this assessment are confidential and not disclosed to anyone.

You can keep coming back to this assessment after a period of time to gauge how you have progressed in terms of your results.
